About Us

Unmatched beauty and hospitality of the Free State, Northern Cape and Limpopo

Letsa Safaris

Letsa Safaris was started by André and Tarina out of their love for nature, wildlife, people and a passion for hunting. With their personal touch, hospitality and attention to detail, you will be sure to have a great time and share a lifetime of memories with your friends and family. Having professionally hunted all over South Africa, we know which area(s) produces the best quality trophies that you wish to hunt and plan according to that. When you choose to go on a Hunting Safari it is arguably a difficult decision to make and a big financial commitment, that is why we offer various Safari options that will suite your needs, requirements and budget.

André has hunted exotic species, plains game and dangerous game extensively throughout South Africa and Namibia and has over 20 years experience. His passion for the outdoors and conservation led him to obtain a diploma in Field Guiding and Game Ranging and specializes in the Big 5. André enjoys meeting hunters from all around the globe and making sure they have the ultimate hunting experience.

Family Values & Hospitality

We are a family run outfit and believe that there is no better place to bond and make new memories than on a Hunting Safari, sitting underneath the starlit sky next to a crackling fire and sharing stories of the day's hunt. Bring the family or your friends and make memories that will last a lifetime.

African Cuisine

Enjoy authentic African cuisine during your stay with us. All of our meals are skillfully prepared using only the best and freshest ingredients.

Accommodation

Our accommodation varies from comfortable to luxurious and all our camps are fitted with the amenities that you would expect on Safari. You can be sure to have a wonderful and peaceful night's sleep ready for the next day packed full of adventure.
