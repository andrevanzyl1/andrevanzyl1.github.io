Hunting

South Africa offers the most diverse range of species found anywhere in the world

Make it your hunt

Operating in the Free State, Northern Cape and Limpopo Provinces of South Africa for over two decades, Letsa Safaris can cater to every preference and need that our clients might have. Pushing the status quo, we believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience a African Hunting Safari without "breaking the bank", that is why we have a wide variety of options available to our clients, from a 5-star luxury Safari, right down to hunting like a local. There are no hidden costs associated with any of our hunts and we pride ourselves on being honest and ethical.

Hunting in a Big 5 Game Reserve

Hunting on a +21700 acre property amongst Cape Buffalo, Elephant, Hippo and Rhino is the ultimate hunting safari. The dense bush and Acacia Thorn bushes are home to a variety of animals. The scenery is breathtaking and this hunting safari will leave you wanting for more. It's the perfect combination of luxury and African hunting. For our non-hunters, we have other activities like game drives, spa treatments, and elephant and lion interactions.

Hunting in the Free State

Our Free State camp is also our main hunting camp, and it is very comfortable and you feel right at home the moment you arrive. We conduct our hunting on two different properties and both have an abundance of game endemic to the area. Besides hunting, you can also enjoy some fishing at the damn while we have sundowners. The terrain is typically open plains with Blesbuck, Springbuck, Gemsbuck (Oryx), Cape Eland and many more roaming this beautiful ranch.

Hunting in Limpopo #1

If you looking for something more luxurious, then this camp is just for you. We have luxury tents that sleep 2 people each or chalets that sleep 4 (a double bed and a bunk bed for the children). You can also take a swim in the pool to cool off after a hard day's hunting while we start the fire in the boma. The landscape is filled with dense bushes, Acacia Thorn bushes and mountains where you will find plenty of Impala, Zebra, Blue Wildebeest, Buffalo and many more roaming on the property.

Hunting in Limpopo #2

This is another gem of a property and you can be sure to have a wonderful time here. It is located close to O.R Tambo International Airport and perfect for a Safari when you have time constraints. The dense bush makes for a perfect stalk while hunting for Zebra, Warthog, Blue Wildebeest, Impala, Nyala or the elusive "Grey Ghost" Kudu.
