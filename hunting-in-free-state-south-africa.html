Hunting in the Free State

Our Free State camp is also our main hunting camp, and it is very comfortable and you feel right at home the moment you arrive. We conduct our hunting on two different properties, 2270 acres with and additional 5000 acres to your disposal if required. Both properties have an abundance of game endemic to the area.

Hunting in the Free State gives hunters the opportunity to hunt magnificent Duiker, Steenbuck, Blesbuck, Gemsbuck (Oryx), Lechwe and Fallow Deer to name just a few. Sable and Roan antelope has also been successfully introduced to the area and produce top quality trophies year after year.
