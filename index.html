Authentic African Hunting Safaris

Letsa Safaris is an established company that offers Hunting Safaris in South Africa and operates in the Free State, Northern Cape and Limpopo provinces. We would like to invite you and your family to join us on an unforgettable hunting adventure. Our various hunting areas are breathtaking and you will be guaranteed to make memories of a lifetime. We offer affordable hunting safaris to both local and international hunters who wish to make their dreams come true and experience the ultimate hunting safari.

Hunting Safaris in South Africa
