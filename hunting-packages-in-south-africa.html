We have a wide variety of packages available to suite any of your needs and budget

What is included

  • The service of a fully qualified Professional Hunter
  • Tracking, skinning and field preparation of your trophies
  • Transportation of your trophies to the taxidermy
  • The use of fully equipped hunting vehicle(s)
  • Laundry service
  • Full accommodation, meals and drinks (water, soft drinks, local beer and wine, etc.)
  • Local liquor in moderation
  • 15% government required tax (VAT)

What is not included

  • Trophy fees on all animals taken or wounded
  • License fees where required
  • TOPS and CITES permits where required
  • Taxidermy and shipping costs
  • Any international or domestic air travel
  • Gratuities for safari staff and Professional Hunters
  • Meals and liquor outside of camps and hunting areas
  • Accommodation prior to and after the hunting safari
  • Personal expenses or phone calls
  • Rifle hire and ammunition per safari if required
  • Excursions outside the contracted safari period
  • Any hardtack liquor like Gin, Whisky, etc. Please arrange ahead of time

Terms and Conditions

  • A deposit of 50% of the total daily rates for the duration of the safari is required to confirm the booking
  • In the case of a packaged hunt, the remaining total cost of the hunt is to be paid 2 weeks prior to arrival. If you do not manage to harvest the animal in the package, 50% of the cost of the animal will be credited back to you
  • Trophy fees are payable on all animals killed or wounded and lost, prior to departure
  • Deposits are not refundable unless prior arrangements were made
  • All payments are to be made in cash or wire transfer only
  • Every reasonable precaution to ensure the safety and good health of clients will be taken. However, no liability will be accepted for injury, loss or illness, before, during or after the safari
  • Prices may be subject to change due to fluctuations in exchange rates. Clients will be notified in advance
