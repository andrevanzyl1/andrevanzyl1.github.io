Hunting In South Africa

South Africa offers the most diverse range of species found anywhere in the world

Make it your hunt

Letsa Safaris offers Hunting Safaris in South Africa and operates in the Free State, Northern Cape and Limpopo provinces for over two decades. With over +500,000 acres of available hunting grounds, we cater to every preference and need that our clients might have. Pushing the status quo, we believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience an true African Hunting Safari, that is why we have a wide variety of options available to our clients, from a 5-star luxury Safari, right down to hunting like a local. There are no hidden costs associated with any of our hunts and we pride ourselves on being honest and ethical

Hunting in the Heart of the Limpopo Province

This beautiful property embodies the meaning of hunting in the Limpopo Province of South Africa. With a touch of style, luxury and great hospitality you will have an amazing time hunting here. The thick bushveld and thorn trees are home to diverse game species endemic to the area like Kudu, the Cape Buffalo, Zebra, Blue Wildebeest, Cape Eland and many more. This is a hunters' paradise waiting to be explored.

Hunting in a Big 5 Game Reserve

Hunting on a +21700 acre property amongst Cape Buffalo, Elephant, Hippo and Rhino is the ultimate hunting safari. The dense bush and Acacia Thorn bushes are home to a variety of animals. The scenery is breathtaking and this hunting safari will leave you wanting for more. It's the perfect combination of luxury and African hunting. For our non-hunters, we have other activities like game drives, spa treatments, and elephant and lion interactions.

Hunting in the Free State

Our Free State camp is also our main hunting camp, and it is very comfortable and you feel right at home the moment you arrive. We conduct our hunting on two different properties and both have an abundance of game endemic to the area. Besides hunting, you can also enjoy some fishing at the damn while we have sundowners. The terrain is typically open plains with Blesbuck, Springbuck, Gemsbuck (Oryx), Cape Eland and many more roaming this beautiful ranch.

Hunting in Limpopo in the Waterberg

If you looking for something more private and luxurious, then this camp is just for you. We have 4 luxury tents that sleep 2 people each and 3 chalets that sleep 4 (a double bed and a bunk bed for the children). You can also take a swim in the pool to cool off after a hard day's hunting while we start the fire in the boma. The landscape is filled with dense bushes, Acacia Thorn bushes and mountains where you will find plenty of Impala, Zebra, Blue Wildebeest, Buffalo and many more roaming on the property.

Hunting in Limpopo South

This is another gem of a property and you can be sure to have a wonderful time here. It is located close to O.R Tambo International Airport and perfect for a Safari when you have time constraints. The dense bush makes for a perfect stalk while hunting for Zebra, Warthog, Blue Wildebeest, Impala, Nyala or the elusive "Grey Ghost" Kudu.

One Day Hunting Trips

While you are on vacation, a business trip or a "stopover," why not join us for a day or two and hunt some of the iconic South African species like Blesbuck, Gemsbuck (Oryx) or Springbuck, to name a few. If you have a specific animal in mind, please let us know so that we can make it happen for you.

Family Safaris

We are a family-orientated outfit and believe that there is no better place to bond and make new memories than on a Hunting Safari, sitting underneath the starlit sky next to a crackling fire and sharing stories of the day's hunt. Bring the family and make memories that will last a lifetime.

What is included

  • The service of a fully qualified Professional Hunter
  • Tracking, skinning and field preparation of your trophies
  • Transportation of your trophies to the taxidermy
  • The use of fully equipped hunting vehicle(s)
  • Laundry service
  • Full accommodation, meals and drinks (water, soft drinks, local beer and wine, etc.)
  • Local liquor in moderation

What is not included

  • Trophy fees on all animals taken or wounded
  • License fees where required
  • TOPS and CITES permits where required
  • Taxidermy and shipping costs
  • Any international or domestic air travel
  • Gratuities for safari staff and Professional Hunters
  • Meals and liquor outside of camps and hunting areas
  • Accommodation prior to and after the hunting safari
  • Personal expenses or phone calls
  • Rifle hire and ammunition per safari if required
  • Excursions outside the contracted safari period
  • Any hardtack liquor like Gin, Whisky, etc. Please arrange ahead of time
